01. Don't be a jerk about work

Good work takes time, and is usually hard. There are bound to be disagreements whilst building a business or product, but the best work happens when the team works well together regardless.

  • Communicate regularly with your team
  • Speak up when something isn't right
  • Be constructive with criticism
  • Nurture the team, rather than order

Tweet it

02. User needs come first

User objectives are business objectives. Taking shortcuts that impact negatively on the experiences of your users will likely come back to haunt you. People respect companies that respect them.

  • Make performance an objective
  • Prioritise accessibility
  • Speak to your customers
  • Act on feedback properly

Tweet it

03. All together or not at all

Design doesn't happen in a vacuum. Neither does business strategy, hiring, or creating a positive working culture. Keeping these things aligned will help your business to run and scale smoothly.

  • Create collaborative teams
  • Invest in tools and processes
  • Don't look for silver bullets
  • Align business efforts

Tweet it

04. Small details make big pictures

The details that make your product memorable can often get lost in the wider vision. Long-term strategy is important, but it's beneficial to stay on top of small changes that can create big experiences.

  • Create positive experiences
  • Remove friction for the user
  • Give product personality
  • Innovation over imitation
